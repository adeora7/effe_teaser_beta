  • About Effervescence

    After putting up a spectacular show last year, Effervescence is back with a bang! Team Effervescence is proud to introduce to you the Campus Ambassador Program. The program is an exclusive opportunity for current college students to gain valuable experience by acting as a link between the cultural festival and the outside community. It provides a platform for passionate students who are willing to implement creative ideas to promote Effervescence 2016 in their respective college/university. One student will be handpicked from a pool of applicants from every college and trained to communicate and promote the fest in their community.

  • Who can apply?

    Any creative and energetic second year student well connected to the student community in his/her campus. The student must possess good interaction skills and a desire to come up with fresh ideas to improve engagement.

  • How can you benefit from the program?

    Campus Ambassador Program gives you a chance to be a part of this cultural extravanganza.

    • A certificate from Team Effervescence and IIIT Allahabad stating your role in promoting the fest as Campus Ambassador.
    • You will be highlighted as Campus Ambassador on the Effervescence website.
    • Great exposure and an opportunity to be mentored on various topics by Team Effe.
    • Hands on skills – Enhance your leadership and communication skills.
    • The training will provide you a platform to learn and showcase your interpersonal skills not only at your college but as well as at broader forums.
    • The opportunity to make good connections and network with influential people.
  • What would be your duties?

    As a Campus Ambassador of Effervescence '16 you will act as a medium of communication between the Organizing Team of Effervescence and you institute.

    • Act as an interface between Effervescence and your college.
    • Create awareness about the fest as a whole in your respective student community.
    • Publicizing events and competitions taking place at Effervescence 2016 through on campus branding as well as through social media.
    • Organizing and coordinating the student contingent attending Effervescence from your respective college/university.
    • Interact and network with college leaders as well as various college clubs/societies.
    • Communicate with Team Effervescence and send information to and fro.
